What are the birthday flowers for each month?

Every birthday month has a unique flower associated with it. Buying specialized birthday flowers is a meaningful and beautiful way to brighten the birthday girl or guy's day!

Here is each month's birthday flower:

  • January Birthdays - Carnation
  • February Birthdays - Iris and Violet
  • March Birthdays - Daffodil
  • April Birthdays - Daisy and Sweet Pea
  • May Birthdays - Lily of the Valley
  • June Birthdays - Rose
  • July Birthdays - Larkspur and Waterlily
  • August Birthdays - Gladiolus
  • September Birthdays - Aster
  • October Birthdays - Marigold
  • November Birthdays - Mum
  • December Birthdays - Narcissus, Holly, Poinsettia

Whatever birthday flowers you choose, the sweet sentiments behind your thoughtful gift are guaranteed to warm their heart.
